The Tradition

A mattress maker dedicated to evolving the luxury sleep experience.
Kluft Heritage

Since our beginning, we have mastered the luxury mattress. As world-class master craftsmen, we have built on this heritage with our opulent, signature creations. Kluft mattresses have always defined world-class comfort. And Kluft craftsmanship now sets the universal standard for sophistication and refinement.

Kluft Today

Perfection is a journey, not a destination. We push the boundaries of craftsmanship and technology, fusing all-natural materials with a passion for design innovation. Our latest endeavor is the culmination of decades of hard work, knowledge and experience that could only be called by one name, Kluft.
