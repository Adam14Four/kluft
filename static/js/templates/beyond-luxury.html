The Beyond Luxury collection is a marriage of old world artisanship and new world technique. Designed with the aid of a sophisticated body mapping system, it’s the most advanced mattress ever designed. We’ve brought rigorous tailoring with a mind for the details: traditional hand side-stitching, individually inserted hand tufts and an unmatched nested coil system.

The Palais Royal

Made by masters. Fit for a king. The Palais Royale is truly Beyond Luxury. It’s handmade with layer upon layer of the world’s finest materials to provide maximum pressure relief, extraordinary support and unsurpassed comfort. Inside you’ll find luxurious cashmere, Joma wool, prime cotton and Talalay latex, materials that cradle the body in total lavishness.
